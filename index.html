How Tesoro works

Three engagements, in the order a CFO buys them.

01 See

The AI spend audit.

We connect billing and gateway data and hand back one number: what you spend, what it buys, and what it should cost. Delivered in weeks, through people you already know.

02 Save

The routing gateway.

Every request goes to the cheapest model that clears the quality bar — re-decided continuously as rate cards move. Runs on the same router demonstrated at torre.q-starr.app; Tesoro is the CFO door into it. Priced at 20% of verified savings plus a platform fee.

03 Serve

Sovereign capacity.

Regulated workloads only capture open-model savings if the model runs in-jurisdiction. SenseiNode operates that capacity in-region. Residency enforced: LGPD (BR), Ley 25.326 (AR), LFPDPPP (MX), Ley 19.628 (CL), Ley 18.331 (UY).
